I work in the [Intelligent Robotics and Vision Lab](https://irvlutd.github.io/) with Prof. Yu Xiang, on manipulation that has to survive contact with the real world — motion planning and trajectory optimization, learning tasks from human video, and the unglamorous work of figuring out *why* a pipeline that looked fine in sim drops the mug on hardware.

Before this: M.S. in Control & Automation at IIT Delhi, and co-founder at [VECROS](https://www.vecros.com/), where we flew drones through GPS-denied spaces and learned that reactive planners fail in far more creative ways than papers suggest.
